About Us

DONA TRAVEL was established in early 2025 with a vision to become a leading name in the domestic tourism industry, offering Muslim-friendly and inclusive travel experiences for all. We believe that Shariah-compliant tourism not only caters to the needs of Muslim travelers but also provides a comfortable, safe, and enjoyable journey for everyone.

With our guiding concept of “Muslim-Friendly, Friendly for All”, we design travel packages that emphasize: Clean and halal-certified food options Properly scheduled prayer times Activities suitable for families and people from all backgrounds As a travel agency focused on domestic tourism, we highlight the beauty and richness of Peninsular Malaysia, with special attention to cultural heritage, natural landscapes, and community-based experiences. 2025 Focus: Selangor For our first year of operation, we have chosen Selangor as our primary destination, featuring three key attractions that reflect a blend of history, Islamic architecture, and modern adventure:

🏛 Sultan Alam Shah Museum – Discover the history and heritage of Selangor
🕌 Chinese Muslim Mosque Klang – A symbol of cultural harmony and Islamic architectural beauty
🧗 Zipline Shah Alam – A thrilling adventure in the heart of the city

In addition to Selangor, we also offer a variety of travel packages across other exciting destinations in Peninsular Malaysia, including Perak, Melaka, Negeri Sembilan, and more — each thoughtfully curated to deliver meaningful, ethical, and memorable travel experiences.

We are committed to delivering travel experiences that are high-quality, ethical, and rooted in local values, while promoting the beauty of Malaysia to Malaysians.
